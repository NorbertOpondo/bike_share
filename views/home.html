MMU BIKE SHARE PROGRAMME

The MMUBike program is a bike share system in place to encourage physical exercise and provide reliable means of transport at MMU

HOW IT WORKS

Its simple to rent, just follow the steps below and enjoy




  • REGISTER

    Register as a member
    with your details and
    login to book a bike



  • RELEASE

    Present your student card
    at the station, the bike
    will be availed to you



  • RIDE

    Take your bike and enjoy
    upto the time allocated.
    Return when done



  • RETURN

    When done, return your bike
    and gear to the station.A staff
    member will inspect the bike
